I'm a designer and hacker.
I love building products for the digital age.

Currently I'm doing a graduate degree in the field of Human-Computer Interaction and Design at the University of Twente (Netherlands). Between my bachelor and graduate studies I worked as a designer and web developer for one and a half years. So far, I've been living in Stockholm, London and Cologne and studied at four different universities. My academic interests include natural language processing, data visualization and multimodal interaction. In the little spare time that's left, I read, cook, travel and photograph.
