I'm a designer, hacker and entrepreneur.
And a global citizen.

For the past year I've been living in Stockholm doing a graduate degree at the Royal Institute of Technology in the field of Human-Computer Interaction. Before that I worked as a designer and front-end developer on a freelance basis in my hometown Cologne, Germany. My two main clients were a global PR agency and medium-sized online marketing agency. From the age of 17 I've been developing websites professionally and since then my interest extended to other fields, such as Multimodal Interaction, Speech and Language Processing and Entrepreneurship.
